Chef John's Chicken Tortilla Soup

Description

A smoky,spicy, slowly simmered tomato chicken broth made from scratch topped with a mix of toasted tortilla strips, sour cream, avocado, cilantro, lima, and crumbled mexican cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 1/2) pound whole chicken
  • 3 White onions, halved, divided
  • 1 bunch cilantro, rinsed well
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon whoile black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 2 dried chili pepper pods,seeded(optional)
  • 3 quarts water

    • Tortilla Strips:

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

    • Charred Vegetable Salsa:

  • 3 large tomatoes
  • 1 sweet red pepper, halved and seeded
  • 1 large or 2 small jalapeno peppers, halved and seeded
  • 6 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 whole canned chipotle pepper in adobo sauce (optional)

    • To Finish:

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 lime, juiced

    • Garnishes:

  • sour cream
  • sliced avacado
  • chopped cilantro
  • shredded or crumbled mexican cheesem such as cotija
  • lime wedges
  • sprinkle of cayenne pepper
