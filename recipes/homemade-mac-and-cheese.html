Homemade Mac And Cheese

Description

A wonderful comfort food. We effortlessly make the cheese from scratch starting with a roux and adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan to make a delicious sauce for our noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of uncooked macaroni noodles
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk, use whole milk for the richest flavor and texture
  • Cheddar and Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and pepper for the sauce, paprika over the topping
  • Bread crumbs, store bought is fine, or you can make your own
